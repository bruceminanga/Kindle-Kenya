Who We Are

Kindle is a national, non-partisan 501c organization that is building a movement of young men and women who are passionate, ready and eager to become the next generation of political leaders, human rights advocates, professionals and entrepreneurs.

Our focus is on training young adults to discover their full potential in political power, human rights advocacy, and entrepreneurship. We are geared to become the nation's leading organization harnessing political ambition, human rights advocacy, professionalism, and entrepreneurship skills.
